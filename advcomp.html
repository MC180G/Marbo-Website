CHARACTERISTICS

Liquid semipermanent release agents that do not transfer onto molded items, approved by Boeing and NASA, for use in high technology areas such as: Aerospace, Telecommunications, Formula 1 racing, sport goods (skis, tennis racquets, surfboards, bicycles), etc..

Marbocote® can be used to mold epoxy, acrylic, phenolic and polyester resins, thermosetting resins, thermoplastic resins and all other types of elastomers reinforced with aramid, carbonfibre and fiberglass.

Marbolube® can be used as an internal release agent for the manufacture of pultruded items in composite materials.

BENEFITS

  • High temperature resistance (over 752° F).
  • Multiple release capability.
  • Reduced labor costs.
  • No mold build up.
Aerospace Applications

Marbocote® are a range of release agents developed for the manufacture of composite items Marbocote® are specially designed to avoid transfer onto the molded parts Marbocote® has received Boeing and NASA approvals on the basis of specific tests conducted to verify the absence of any contaminants on the surface of the molded parts

Composite Applications

Marbocote® increases the number of releases achieved per single application These products impart a glossy and semi-matte finish to molded items The application of the Marbocote® sealer eliminates release problems due to the porosity of resin or aluminium new molds

Pultrusion

Marbolube® U 100 is an internal release agent for pultruded articles manufactured in composite materials
