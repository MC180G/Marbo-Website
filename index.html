Welcome, thank you for visiting Marbo America Inc! We are the distribution and support resource for Marbocote™ specialty chemicals in North America.

What We Do

Marbo America Inc, part of the Marbo Group, is a U.S. based company founded in 2002 to supply semi-permanent release products for use in the epoxy, phenolic, rubber, thermosetting resins, rotomolding thermoplastic resins, high performances composites, pultrusion, GRP (glass reinforced polyester) and concrete industries, as well as for the die lubricants and polyurethane industries.

Marbo has built a world wide reputation for quality and performance and has probably the broadest range of release products of any manufacturer in the world today.
